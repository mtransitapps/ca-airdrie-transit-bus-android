This app adds Airdrie Transit buses information to MonTransit.

This app provides the buses schedule as well as the latest news from @AirdrieTransit on Twitter.

Airdrie Transit buses serve Airdrie & Calgary in Alberta, Canada.

Once this application is installed, the MonTransit app will display buses information (schedule...).

This application only has a temporary icon: download the MonTransit app (free) in the "More ..." section bellow or by following this Google Play link https://bit.ly/MonTransitPlay

You can install this application on the SD card but it is not recommended.

The information comes from the GTFS file provided by the Airdrie Transit on the Calgary Region Open Data website.
http://www.calgaryregionopendata.ca/browse/file/10090

This application is free and open-source:
https://github.com/mtransitapps/ca-airdrie-transit-bus-android

This app is not related with Airdrie Transit or Calgary Region.

Permissions:
- Other: required to news from Twitter
